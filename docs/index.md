# Welcome to OrbitalPlay

OrbitalPlay is an interactive educational platform designed to make quantum chemistry accessible and intuitive for undergraduates.

## The Gap in Science Education

Modern quantum chemistry tools like Gaussian, ORCA, or NWChem are powerful but have steep learning curves. They are often intimidating for students who are just beginning to learn about molecular orbitals and chemical bonding.

OrbitalPlay bridges this gap by providing a **Visual Orbital Engine**.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Molecular Playground:** Real-time visualization of molecular orbitals as you move atoms.
- **Visual Bonding & Anti-bonding:** Easily identify nodes, phase changes, and probability densities.
- **On-the-fly Calculations:** Powered by Hartree-Fock theory to ensure scientific accuracy while maintaining responsiveness.
- **Educational UI:** Focused on student intuition rather than complex command-line interfaces.

## Our Mission

To transform how students perceive and understand the quantum world by turning abstract equations into interactive, visual experiences.
